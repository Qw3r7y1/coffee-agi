# MAILLARD3 Brand Identity Specifications

## Logo
- **Primary Mark**: Teardrop/water drop silhouette in black
- **Wordmark**: "MAILLARD3" in custom serif typeface
- **Versions Shown**: 
  - Stacked vertical arrangement
  - Horizontal arrangement
  - Mark only (teardrop symbol)
  - Monochromatic variations

## Color Palette
- **Primary Black**: Used for logo, typography, primary elements
- **Secondary Colors**: Multi-color palette visible in product/flavor applications (reds, oranges, yellows, greens)
- **Neutral**: White/off-white for backgrounds

## Typography
- **Primary Typeface**: Custom serif font (for "MAILLARD3" wordmark)
- **Secondary Typeface**: Sans-serif for body copy and descriptive text
- **Hierarchy**: 
  - Display/Logo use
  - Body/descriptive text (smaller scale)

## Packaging & Layout
- **Modular Grid System**: Visible dot/circle grid pattern used throughout packaging design
- **Product Format**: Small rectangular boxes/containers
- **Labeling Hierarchy**: Brand mark prominent, descriptive text below
- **Tag Line/Description**: "distinctive flavor portfolio" visible on packaging

## Brand Attributes
- **Tone**: Minimal, sophisticated, systematic
- **Visual System**: Geometric, grid-based dot pattern system
- **Product Category**: Specialty flavoring/culinary product

## Applications Shown
- Product packaging boxes
- Tote bags/merchandise
- T-shirts
- Digital/web presentations
- Retail environment photography

# MAILLARD Brand Identity Specifications

## Logo
- **Primary Mark**: Stylized "M" with droplet/teardrop form
- **Versions Shown**: 
  - Monochromatic (white)
  - Full color
  - Stacked lockup with "MAILLARD" wordmark
  - Horizontal lockup variations

## Color Palette
- **Primary Teal/Blue-Green**: Used throughout branding
- **Warm Gold/Yellow**: Accent color (visible on product packaging)
- **White**: Primary background and contrast
- **Charcoal/Dark Gray**: Secondary color for text and depth
- **Forest Green**: Visible in photography and background elements

## Typefaces
- **Primary Wordmark**: Custom or modified sans-serif (appears geometric and modern)
- Usage: Logo, primary branding applications

## Packaging Design
- **Product Format**: Glass jars with labels
- **Label Hierarchy**: 
  - Brand name "MAILLARD" prominent
  - Product information secondary
  - Clean, minimal text approach
- **Three product variations shown**: Different colored accent bands/labels
- **Material**: Clear glass containers

## Design Applications
- Business cards
- Clothing/merchandise (t-shirts with logo)
- Vehicles (delivery van with branding)
- Interior signage
- Digital/web mockups
- Printed collateral
- Stickers and promotional items

## Layout & Spacing
- Generous white space around logo
- Minimal, clean aesthetic throughout
- Organized grid-based presentation

## Tone & Brand Values
- Modern, minimal, clean aesthetic
- Professional yet approachable
- Focus on natural/organic imagery (foliage, natural elements)
