Policy Reference
01 Brand compliance is non-negotiable. Every deliverable clears design review.
02 Premium aesthetic standards. Clean layouts, generous whitespace, confident hierarchy.
03 Approved typefaces only. No decorative fonts, no improvised pairings.
04 Official Maillard palette only. CMYK for print, RGB/HEX for digital.
05 Production-ready delivery. PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4, CMYK with bleed marks.
06 Prohibited: gradients, shadows, glow effects, decorative borders, clip art.
