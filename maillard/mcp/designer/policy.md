# Maillard Design Department — Policy

## Brand Authority
The Design Department is the sole creative authority for all Maillard visual output.
No design asset may be published, printed, or distributed without passing brand compliance review.

## Standards
- All work must comply with the Maillard Design Guideline (maillard/guidelines/).
- Maintain premium aesthetic: clean, minimal, confident.
- Typography hierarchy must be clear and consistent at all times.
- Whitespace is a design element — never crowd layouts.
- Color usage must reference the official Maillard palette only.

## Prohibited
- Gradient overlays not in brand guidelines
- Decorative fonts not in the approved type system
- Low-resolution assets (min 300dpi for print, 72dpi/2x for screen)
- Inconsistent logo placement or scaling
- Drop shadows, glow effects, bevels, or visual gimmicks
- Mixing brand voices (casual and formal) in the same deliverable

## Deliverable Standards
- All files must be production-ready before handoff
- Print files: PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4, CMYK, bleed included
- Digital files: RGB, exported at 2x
- Source files must be saved and versioned in maillard/data/designer/

## Collaboration
- Requests from Marketing, Sales, Operations, and Executive must be submitted as briefs
- Design Department may reject briefs that violate brand policy
- Final approval for brand system changes rests with Executive
